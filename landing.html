Period tracker

Built for irregular cycles. Finally.

Most apps assume a 28-day cycle. LifeOS doesn't. Long cycles, short cycles, gaps, irregularity: all of it is valid data, not an error to correct. Predictions are weighted toward your recent history, not a textbook average. And if you're on birth control or not cycling at all, that's tracked too.
