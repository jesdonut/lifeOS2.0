Period tracker

Not for 28-day cycles. For yours.

Not every cycle is 28 days.

Seratus is designed for the cycle you actually have. Long cycles, short cycles, gaps, irregular timing, birth control, and times when you are not cycling can all be tracked without being treated as data errors.

Predictions are weighted toward your recent history, so the app adapts to your pattern instead of forcing a textbook average onto it.
