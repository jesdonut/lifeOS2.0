Period tracker

Not for 28-day cycles. For yours.

Most apps assume a 28-day cycle. Seratus doesn't. Long cycles, short cycles, gaps, irregularity: all of it is valid data, not an error to correct. Predictions are weighted toward your recent history, not a textbook average. And if you're on birth control or not cycling at all, that's tracked too.
