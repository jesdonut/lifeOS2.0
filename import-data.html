favorite Apple Health

Imports period and cycle data only: flow, spotting, ovulation tests, BBT, cervical mucus, and contraceptives.

Step 1 — Export from your iPhone
  1. Open the Health app on your iPhone.
  2. Tap your profile picture in the top right.
  3. Scroll down and tap Export All Health Data.
  4. Tap Export and save or AirDrop the ZIP file to your computer.
Step 2 — Extract the ZIP file
Double-click the ZIP file in Finder. It will extract to a folder called apple_health_export. Open that folder and find export.xml.
Right-click the ZIP file and choose Extract All. Open the extracted folder apple_health_export and find export.xml.
Run unzip export.zip in your terminal. Open the extracted folder apple_health_export and find export.xml.
Step 3 — Upload export.xml
upload_file

Only export.xml is accepted. No ZIP files.
