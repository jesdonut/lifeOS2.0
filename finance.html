Finance

Your money, all of it.

Expenses and income, foreign currency holdings, and investments. Three sub-modules, one place.

Expenses  In progress

Track income and spending month by month. Fields adapt to your country's tax and deduction structure: salary, transport reimbursements, insurance, pension, and more. Daily spending from your calendar flows in automatically.

Currency  In progress

Log foreign currency lots and track exchange rates from any source, manual entry or automatic. See your gain or loss in your base currency at a glance. Built around JPY and IDR, but not limited to them.

Investments  Coming soon

Savings accounts, fixed deposits, bonds, and pension contributions across banks and countries, all in one view. Country-specific products like NISA (Japan) and Reksadana (Indonesia) are tracked under the same roof.
