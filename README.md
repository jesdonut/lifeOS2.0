# LifeOS 2.0

A personal life operating system built to last 100 years — from the year you're born.

Set your birth year, timezone, and currencies once. The app adapts to your life.

---

## Modules

- **Calendar** — week, month, year views with drag and drop
- **Period tracker** — optional. Designed specifically for irregular cycles. Backend-first.
- **Finance** — monthly income and expense tracker
- **Bank** — account balances and transfers
- **Currency** — multi-currency rates, lot tracking, P&L
- **Investments** — NISA, government bonds, time deposits
- **Savings** — accounts and deposits
- **Notes** — sidebar notes and countdowns

---
