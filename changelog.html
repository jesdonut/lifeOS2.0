Roadmap

What Seratus can do now, what is being built, and what comes next. A personal project moves at its own pace.

Live 2026
Calendar Month and week views. Events with 9 built-in categories and drag-and-drop between days. Daily spend tracked per category. Park unscheduled events to the notes sidebar for later.
Period tracker Year overview with 12 month calendars. Daily logger for flow, symptoms, and BBT. Cycle stats with EWMA-weighted predictions, fertile window, and ovulation estimates. Month detail panel with BBT chart and symptom summary.
Notes Collapsible sidebar available from every screen. Free-form notes and a parking lot for unscheduled events.
Import Apple Health XML parser: extracts period flow, spotting, BBT, discharge, ovulation, and contraceptive records. Parsed entirely on your device.
Settings Profile, timezone, calendar category editor, appearance, full data export and import, log out.
In progress
Calendar Year view with monthly overviews, goals, and age display. Week start day setting.
Finance Income and expense tracking. Country-adaptive fields for Japan: salary, health insurance, pension, resident tax. Daily calendar spending flows in automatically.
Next
Bank and savings Multi-country, multi-currency account balances. Fixed deposits, bonds, NISA (Japan), and pension contributions tracked in one place.
Currency and FX Lot tracking for JPY and IDR. Exchange rate logging and P&L in base currency.
Later
Import Clue JSON, Natural Cycles CSV, Flo JSON, and Seratus v1 JSON.
Period Birth control logging. Travel date adjustments for cycle predictions.
Calendar Recurring events. Goals with progress tracking.
v1, the original 2023 – 2025

The first version: a single-file app, never shared, never meant to be. Calendar, period tracker, finance, bank, savings, deposits, NISA, bonds, currency lot tracking, notes. All in one ~2,500-line JS file. It worked. v2 is a clean rewrite of everything it got right, with what it got wrong fixed from the start.
